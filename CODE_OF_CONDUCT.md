# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We are committed to making participation in this project a harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

**Expected behavior:**
- Be respectful and constructive in all interactions
- Accept feedback gracefully and offer it thoughtfully
- Focus on what is best for the project and its users
- Show empathy toward other contributors

**Unacceptable behavior:**
- Harassment, insults, or derogatory comments
- Personal or political attacks
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Any conduct that would be considered inappropriate in a professional setting

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported by opening a [GitHub Issue](https://github.com/giladresisi/ai-dev-env/issues) or contacting the maintainer directly. All reports will be reviewed and investigated, and a response will be provided appropriate to the circumstances.
